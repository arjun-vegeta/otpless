# How to Activate Pro Plan

> Activating the Pro Plan on OTPless is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to get started:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to OTPless">
    Begin by logging in to [OTPless.com](https://otpless.com) with your registered email ID.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Application">
    Select the application where you'd like to activate the Pro Plan from your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Connectors">
    Go to the **Connectors** section within your selected application.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Locate the OTPless Pro Plan">
    In the **Connectors** section, find the OTPless Pro Plan and click on it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate the Pro Plan">
    Click the **Activate** button to enable the Pro Plan for your application.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Ensure you have sufficient balance in your OTPless wallet to successfully activate the plan.</Note>
